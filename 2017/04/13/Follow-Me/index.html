Follow Me

发表于 2017-04-13 | 分类于 writings |

《攻壳机动队：无罪》，神作，毫无疑问。

片尾的《Follow Me》就是那个草薙素子存在的世界啊。

Follow me
跟着我

to a land across the shining sea
到那闪耀之海的彼岸

Waiting beyond the world that we have known
守候那超越一切已知的世界

Beyond the world that dream could be
超越一切美梦的世界

and the joy we have tasted
超越那些我们已遍尝的欢愉

Follow me
跟着我

Along the road that only love can see
沿着这唯爱可见的道路

Rising above the fun years of the night
自欢欣之夜的岁月中升华

into the light beyond the tears and all the years we have wasted
融入那驾凌于苦痛和年华虚度的光

Follow me
跟着我

to a distant land this mountain high
前往那遥远大陆的巅峰

where all the music that we always kept inside will fill the sky
在那里 深藏心底的音韵将飘舞天际

Singing in the silent swerve a heart is free
在低吟浅唱中让心自由

while the world goes on turning and turning
世界会继续不停运转

Turning and falling
运转 直至崩溃
